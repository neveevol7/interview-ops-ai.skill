## 🇨🇳 Interview-Ops-AI (面试运营 AI)

> **面试不是一场考试，而是一次基于价值对等的产品交付。**

`Interview-Ops-AI` 是一个基于 AI 驱动的面试深度适配引擎。它将资深产品经理（PM）的思维逻辑与项目管理（NPDP）体系相结合，通过结构化的输入，自动生成一份具备“确定性交付价值”的面试通关攻略。

### 🌟 核心特性
- **ROI 驱动逻辑**：不谈感情，只谈你能为公司节省多少“试错成本”与“磨合时间”。
- **项目对标矩阵**：自动将你的简历项目与 JD 关键词进行 STAR 匹配。
- **动态薪资博弈**：根据当前薪资与 JD 范围，自动计算谈判筹码与话术。
- **全轮次适配**：针对 HR 面、业务面、终面，自动调整输出侧重点。

### 🛠️ 如何使用
1. 复制本仓库中的 `core-engine.md` 内容。
2. 发送给任何支持长文本的 AI（如 Gemini, Claude, GPT-4）。
3. 按照 AI 的引导，依次输入你的简历、JD 及薪资等信息。
4. 获取一份定制化的 `.md` 面试攻略。

## 🇺🇸 Interview-Ops-AI (English Version)

> **An interview is not an exam, but a product delivery based on value equivalence.**

`Interview-Ops-AI` is an AI-driven deep interview adaptation engine. It combines the logic of senior Product Managers (PM) with the New Product Development Professional (NPDP) framework. Through structured inputs, it automatically generates an interview conquest guide with "deterministic delivery value."

### 🌟 Key Features
- **ROI-Driven Logic**: Forget the sentiments; focus on how much "trial-and-error cost" and "onboarding time" you save for the company.
- **Project Alignment Matrix**: Automatically match your resume projects with JD keywords using the STAR method.
- **Dynamic Salary Negotiation**: Calculate negotiation leverage and scripts based on your current salary and the JD range.
- **Full-Round Adaptation**: Automatically adjust output focus for HR, Technical/Business, and Final interviews.

### 🛠️ How to Use
1. Copy the contents of `core-engine.md` in this repository.
2. Send it to any AI supporting long context (e.g., Gemini, Claude, GPT-4).
3. Follow the AI's guidance to input your resume, JD, and salary information.
4. Receive a customized `.md` interview strategy guide.
